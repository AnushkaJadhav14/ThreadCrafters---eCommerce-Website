Returns and Exchange Policy

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If you're not satisfied, you may return or exchange the product within 7 days of receiving your order.

Conditions for Return

The product must be unused, in its original packaging, and with all labels intact. We cannot accept returns for items that have been worn, washed, or damaged.

How to Return Your Item

To initiate a return, please contact our customer support team at returns@threadcrafters.com with your order number and reason for return. We will provide instructions on how to return your product.

Refund Process

Once we receive your return, we will process a refund to your original payment method. Please allow 5-10 business days for the refund to be processed.

Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange an item, please contact us and we will guide you through the process.

Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding our return policy, feel free to reach out to our customer support team at support@threadcrafters.com or contact us using contact page in below footer.
