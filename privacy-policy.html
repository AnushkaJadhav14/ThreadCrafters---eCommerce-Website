Privacy Policy

Introduction

At ThreadCrafters, we respect and protect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information when you create an account, place an order, or sign up for our newsletter. This may include your name, email address, shipping address, payment information, and browsing behavior on our site.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to process orders, improve your shopping experience, and communicate with you about your orders or new products.

Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to ensure your personal data is protected.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. However, we may share your data with third parties to provide services related to your order, such as payment processors and delivery companies.

Contact Us
